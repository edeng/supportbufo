Privacy Policy for Keep Bufo Alive

Last updated: September 4, 2024

1. Information We Collect

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Game Data: In-game progress and achievements.
  • Analytics Data: Usage statistics and performance metrics via GameAnalytics.

We do not collect personal information such as names or email addresses.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Improve game performance and user experience.
  • Provide and maintain game services.
  • Analyze usage patterns and optimize gameplay.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., GameAnalytics for analytics).
  • Unity Technologies for advertising services.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

4. In-App Purchases and Advertising

  • Future in-app purchases will be handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
  • We use Unity Ads for rewarded advertising. Please refer to Unity's Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Data Storage and Security

Currently, game data is stored locally on your device. We plan to implement cloud saves in the future.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You can request to:

  • Access your game data.
  • Delete your local game data by uninstalling the app.

For users in the EU and California, you have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA respectively.

7. Children's Privacy

Our game is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. International Data Transfers

As we operate globally, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Check this page for the latest version.

10. Contact Us

For questions about this policy, contact us at: eden@keepbufoalive.com
